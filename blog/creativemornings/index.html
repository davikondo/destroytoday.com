CreativeMornings

navigateleft January 08, 2014 navigateright

This past December, I spoke at CreativeMornings, a breakfast lecture series for the creative community with 65 chapters worldwide and growing. The New York venue is located several blocks from my co-working space, Studiomates, so I’m often in attendance. This time, however, I was fortunate enough to take the stage. Considering the month’s theme, “Make,” my talk focused on my dad and how he made me into the “maker” I am today.

For an extended transcription of my talk, you can refer to the previous article.
